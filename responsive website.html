KOENISEGG AGERA

The Koenigsegg Agera is a mid-engine sports car produced by Swedish car manufacturer Koenigsegg. It is a successor to the CCX/CCXR. The name comes from the Swedish verb 'agera' which means "to act" or in imperative form "(You) act!".

Koenigsegg Agera Latest Update March 04, 2015: Koenigsegg has revealed its another potent Agera supercar at the on-going Geneva Motor Show. Called the ‘Agera RS,’ the supercar is a stripped-and-whipped version of the successful ‘Agera R,’ and offers a mighty 1160 bhps to be explored on the tarmac.It gets its power from an uprated version of the familiar 5.0-litre V8 mill, which has been optimized for regular pump gas and now puts out a whopping 1,160bhp, as compared to the 960bhp of this car. While the transmission duty is fulfilled by the same 7-speed paddle-shift transmission with auto shift mode Electronic differential. Only 25 examples of this series will be manufactured, out of which 10 have been pre-booked.

The Koenigsegg Agera has 1 Petrol Engine on offer. The Petrol engine is 4695 cc . It is available with the Automatic transmission.
